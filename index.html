4Cast

OUT-COMPUTING POVERTY

Bigger data. Better farming. Less poverty.

4Cast is combining Microsoft technology and big data to bring better weather forecasts to farmers in developing countries.

Anyone with a simple cell phone that has SMS capabilities can freely access 4Cast technology, meaning that we can help farmers even in the world's most impovershed areas. By providing a better understanding of when to plant crops in order to receive the most bountiful harvests, 4Cast is helping to raise many people above the poverty line.

The Problem

Farmers in rural areas rely heavily upon their crops. However, global warming has negatively impacted weather, making it more difficult to predict weather patterns. Lack of technology to predict weather has a massive impact on these farmers.

The 4Cast Solution

4Cast is bringing valuable, and more accurate, weather predictions to farmers in impovershed regions of the world. Since the economies of developing nations consist primarily of agriculture, 4Cast can have a large impact on outsmarting poverty.
